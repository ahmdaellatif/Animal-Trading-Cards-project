Hedwig (Snowy Owl)

The snowy owl is a large, white owl of the true owl family. It is sometimes also referred as the polar owl, the white owl and the Arctic owl.

  • Origin: Arcitic region
  • Average size: 27 inches
  • Winspan: 5o inches
  • Genus: Bubo

Most owls sleep during the day and hunt at night, but the snowy owl is often active during the day, especially in the summertime. The snowy owl is both a specialized and generalist hunter.
